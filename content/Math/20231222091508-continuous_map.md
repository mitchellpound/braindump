# continuous map

# Definition
Informally, continuous functions map opens sets to open sets.
For two [[20231220133911-topology|topological spaces]] $(X, \tau)$ and $(Y, \sigma)$ a map $f: X \to Y$ is continuous if
$$
\forall V \subset Y \ \text{open} \ f^{-1}(V) \in \tau
$$
