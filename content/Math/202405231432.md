# Definition
A **Hamiltonian G-space** is the data $(M, \omega, G, \mu)$ where $(M, \omega)$ is a [[202404062124|symplectic manifold]], with a [[202405231352|Hamiltonian $G$-action]] with [[202405231409|moment map]] $\mu:M \to \mathfrak{g}^{*}$.
