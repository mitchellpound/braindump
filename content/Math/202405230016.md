# Symplectomorphism
A symplectomorphism is a [[202405021216|smooth map]] $F:(M,\omega_1) \to (N,\omega_2)$ from [[202404062124|symplectic manifolds]] that preserves the symplectic structure of both. 
Symbolically, this means
$$F^*\omega_2 = \omega_1$$
