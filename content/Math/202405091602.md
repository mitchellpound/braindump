# Matrix Lie algebra
Since $\text{GL}(n)$ is a Lie group, a **matrix Lie algebra** is any subset $G \subseteq \text{GL}(n)$ that is a Lie subgroup. 

# Why care?
We don't really need to study anything else for the finite dimensional cases.
