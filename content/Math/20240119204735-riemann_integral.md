# Riemann integral
# Definition
$$\int f = \sup\{L(f, P) |\text{P is a partition of }f\}$$

# Note on integrability
A function is Reiman integrable if the [[20240112110909-riemann_lower_upper_sum|Riemann lower and upper sums]] are equal.
