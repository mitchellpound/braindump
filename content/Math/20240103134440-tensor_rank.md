# tensor rank

The rank of the [[20240103134240-tensor_coordinate_matrix|tensor coordinate matrix]] of a tensor.

# Well definedness
For any two given basis for the vector spaces U and V, the tensor coordinate matrix for $z \in U \otimes V$ will have the same rank.
