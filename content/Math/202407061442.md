# Definition
A [[20231220133911-topology|topological space]] $X$ is called **semilocally simply connected** if for $x \in X$, there exists an open set $x \in U \subset X$ such that 

$$\iota_*:\pi_1(U, x_0) \longrightarrow \pi_1(X, x)$$

is the trivial map.
