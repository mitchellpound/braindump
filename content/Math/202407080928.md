# Definition of cycles
Let $A_\bullet$ be a [[202407080919|chain complex]]. 
Then there is a [[202407080913|graded abelian group]] $Z_\bullet(A)$ with 

$$Z_n \coloneqq \ker(d:A_n \to A_{n-1}).$$

These are called **cycles**.
