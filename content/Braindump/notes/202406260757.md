# Definition
A **homogeneous space** of a [[202405061454|Lie group]] $G$ is a [[20231222094707-smooth_manifold|smooth manifold]] $M$ with a differentiable [[202405081602|transitive]] left [[202405081540|action]] of $G$ on $M$.
