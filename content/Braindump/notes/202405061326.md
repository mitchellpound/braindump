# symplectic complement
For a subspace $W$ of a [[202405061301|symplectic vector space]] $V$ with symplectic form $\omega$, the **symplectic complement** is the set 
$$W^\omega = \{v \in V \ | \ \omega(v, w) = 0 \quad \forall w \in W \}$$
