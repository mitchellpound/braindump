# Definition
Let $R$ be a commutative [[202404171457|ring]]. 
An **$R$-algebra** is a ring homomorphism $\alpha:R \to S$ such that $\alpha(R)$ is contained in the center of $S$. 

That is, 
$$
\alpha(r)s = s\alpha(r) \qquad \forall r \in R, s \in S.
$$
