# Definition of boundaries
Let $A_\bullet$ be a [[202407080919|chain complex]]. 
There is a [[202407080913|graded abelian group]] $B_\bullet(A)$ with 


$$B_n \coloneqq \text{im}(d:A_{n+1} \to A_{n}).$$

These are called **boundaries**.
