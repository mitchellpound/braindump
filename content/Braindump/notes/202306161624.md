# Markov chain
A series of data (usually time series). Must have [[202306161618|Markov property]]. 

Transition probabilities cannot change based on time. I.E.
$$P(S_t=q_i|S_{t-1}=q_j) = P(S_{t+1} = q_i|S_t = q_j)$$
